# YouTube Research: I Built an AI Content Agent With N8N and Claude
**Source:** Greg Isenberg (feat. Mr. Vibe Marketing)
**Video Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m9iaJNJE2-M

## Introduction
Greg Isenberg and "Mr. Vibe Marketing" break down a complete end-to-end workflow using **n8n** to automate high-quality B2B content creation. The goal is to move away from "boring AI fluff" and create validated posts that actually drive engagement.

## Key Insights
* **Validated Research Phase:** Instead of guessing, the agent uses **Apify** to scrape top-performing videos from YouTube and posts from X (Twitter). This ensures the AI starts with "context" of what is already working in the market.
* **The "Human-in-the-Loop" Step:** The workflow doesn't post blindly. It sends a draft to **Slack** via a Google Doc link, allowing a human to approve or edit before it goes live on LinkedIn.
* **Advanced Prompting with Claude 3.7 Sonnet:** The team uses Claude 3.7 for its superior writing style, specifically instructing it to avoid "M-dashes" and corporate jargon, while adopting a "Creator" persona rather than a corporate one.
* **Multi-LLM Strategy via Open Router:** * **Perplexity:** Used for real-time research and fact-checking (stats/trends).
    * **Claude:** Used for the final creative writing and brand voice alignment.
    * **OpenAI:** Used for image generation (DALL-E).
* **Efficiency Gains:** This setup is claimed to save **10-15 hours per week** by automating the research, ideation, drafting, and distribution phases of social media marketing.
