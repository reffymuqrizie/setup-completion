# YouTube Research: How To Create a B2B Content Strategy that ACTUALLY Converts In 2026
**Source:** Ben B2B

## Introduction
Ben challenges the "Content is King" cliché, arguing that in B2B marketing, **Strategy is the King** and **Distribution is the Queen**. Using a chess analogy, he explains how to navigate macro/micro distractions to reach the final decision-maker.

## Key Insights
* **The Chessboard Framework:** Content marketing isn't just about posting; it’s about navigating barriers like macro factors (inflation/politics), micro factors (competitors), and internal resource constraints.
* **Strategy vs. Content:** "Random moves" (posting without a plan) won't win the game. A real strategy bridges the gap between content and business goals through messaging and credibility.
* **Four Messaging Pillars:**
    1. **Angle:** Your unique stance/opinion on the industry.
    2. **Application:** Showing exactly how your product solves a problem.
    3. **Advocacy:** Using case studies and testimonials as proof.
    4. **Ask:** High-intent content like landing pages and CTAs.
* **Distribution is Queen:** The most powerful piece. You must choose the right channels (LinkedIn, Search, Podcasts) based on where your ICP actually consumes information, not just where it's easiest to post.
