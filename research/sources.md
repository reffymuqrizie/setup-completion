# Research Sources: YouTube Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

This document lists the experts and practitioners selected for researching effective YouTube strategies specifically for the B2B SaaS sector. These sources were chosen based on their expertise in community-led growth, B2B sales, and digital marketing authority.

### Selected Experts & Practitioners

1. **Sam Oh (Ahrefs)**
   * **Source:** [Ahrefs YouTube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@AhrefsCom)
   * **Annotation:** A premier example of "Product-Led Content." Sam Oh demonstrates how to integrate SaaS tools into educational tutorials that rank high on search results.

2. **Greg Isenberg**
   * **Source:** [Greg Isenberg Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@GregIsenberg)
   * **Annotation:** Focuses on community-led growth and building "unsexy" businesses. His video strategy is excellent for understanding B2B positioning and niche community building.

3. **HubSpot Marketing**
   * **Source:** [HubSpot CRM Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@HubSpot-CRM)
   * **Annotation:** A global leader in inbound marketing. This channel provides a masterclass on how to scale B2B educational content and maintain a consistent brand voice.

4. **Alex Hormozi**
   * **Source:** [Alex Hormozi Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@AlexHormozi)
   * **Annotation:** Expert in high-impact sales messaging and business scaling. His high-retention editing style and psychological approach to content offer deep insights into B2B lead generation.

5. **Rob Walling (Startups for the Rest of Us)**
   * **Source:** [Rob Walling Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@RobWalling)
   * **Annotation:** A veteran in the Micro-SaaS space. His content focuses on the technical and strategic aspects of bootstrapping and growing SaaS companies without VC funding.

6. **Sam Dunning**
   * **Source:** [Sam Dunning Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@SamDunning)
   * **Annotation:** Focuses specifically on B2B SEO and website conversion. His channel is a great resource for understanding how YouTube content can directly feed a B2B sales funnel.

7. **Charlie Chang**
   * **Source:** [Charlie Chang Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@CharlieChang)
   * **Annotation:** Expert in personal branding and financial education. His content provides insights into how SaaS founders can leverage personal authority to drive business growth.

8. **Neil Patel**
   * **Source:** [Neil Patel Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@neilpatel)
   * **Annotation:** One of the most recognized voices in digital marketing. He excels at breaking down complex SEO and content marketing strategies into actionable video format for businesses.

9. **Ben B2B**
   * **Source:** [Ben B2B Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@benb2b)
   * **Annotation:** Specializes in B2B marketing strategies and LinkedIn-YouTube cross-pollination. Excellent for seeing how to structure professional, high-converting B2B content.

10. **Instantly.ai**
    * **Source:** [Instantly.ai Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@InstantlyAI/)
    * **Annotation:** A rapidly growing SaaS company using YouTube for "Building in Public" and providing extreme value through cold email masterclasses, directly driving product sign-ups.
