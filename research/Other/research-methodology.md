# Research Methodology & AI Tooling

To ensure the high speed and accuracy of this repository, a specialized AI-assisted research workflow was implemented. This demonstrates the ability to leverage modern LLMs for complex data synthesis.

##  Internal Tooling Stack
* **Primary AI:** **Gemini 3 Flash** (Google)
    * Used for multi-step reasoning, cross-referencing expert insights, and synthesizing raw transcripts into structured B2B strategies.
    * Leveraged for its high-context window to analyze long-form marketing content.
* **Extraction:** **YoutubeTranscript.io**
    * Used to bypass manual note-taking and extract 100% accurate raw text from technical marketing videos.
* **Validation:** Manual cross-referencing with expert LinkedIn profiles and official websites to ensure "Search/AEO" trends were current for 2026.

##  The "Synthesis" Workflow
1. **Extraction:** Raw transcripts were pulled from high-authority sources via YoutubeTranscript.io.
2. **Contextual Analysis:** Gemini was used to identify "Golden Nuggets"—specific tactics that move the needle for B2B SaaS.
3. **Drafting:** Insights were formatted into a standardized Markdown structure for easy repository navigation.
