# LinkedIn Post RResearch: $4M Content Strategy for 2025
**Author:** Alex Hormozi
**Topic:** Scaling B2B Content via Volume and Distribution
**Source Link:** [Alex Hormozi LinkedIn Post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/alexhormozi_my-4000000-content-strategy-for-2025-activity-7251286824633970688-maaR/)

## Key Insights from the Post
In this post, Alex Hormozi breaks down his $4 million investment into content strategy for 2025. The core focus is on:

1. **Volume as a Competitive Advantage:** Moving from 80 pieces of content per week to over 200.
2. **Platform Specificity:** Tailoring content for LinkedIn, YouTube, and X to match how users consume information on each platform.
3. **The "Value Loop":** Using free content to solve specific problems for the "Problem Aware" audience, which eventually converts them into high-intent leads for his B2B portfolio.
4. **Data-Driven Iteration:** Using engagement metrics to decide which topics deserve a deep-dive YouTube video or a high-production series.

## Brief Annotation & Analysis
* **Relevance to B2B SaaS:** This strategy is highly applicable for SaaS companies looking to dominate their niche. By increasing volume, a SaaS brand stays "top of mind" throughout the long B2B buying cycle.
* **Technical Takeaway:** He treats content production like a factory (systematized), which is a key trait of successful "Growth Engines" in the SaaS industry.
